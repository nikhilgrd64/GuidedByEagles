Nutrition for Adventure Travel

✦ Fuel Your Journey

Eating the right food boosts stamina, focus, and recovery. Here’s how to stay fueled:

  • Pre-Trip: Carb-load 2-3 days before for energy stores
  • During Trip: Snack every 2 hours (nuts, dry fruits, protein bars)
  • Post-Trip: Focus on proteins and hydration

✦ Hydration Tips

  • Carry a 2L water bladder or filtered bottle
  • Add electrolyte tablets on hot or high-altitude routes
  • Track intake: ~3-4L/day average during active travel

✦ Travel-Friendly Foods

  • Energy bars, trail mix, peanut butter sachets
  • Instant oats, noodles, canned tuna or beans
  • ORS packets, dark chocolate, dried fruits
