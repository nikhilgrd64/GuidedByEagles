From rocky trails to forest roads, every terrain demands a different approach:

  • Gravel: Keep a light grip and use steady throttle.
  • Sand: Shift your weight back, keep momentum steady.
  • Hills: Downshift before ascent, use engine braking on descent.
  • Wet Roads: Avoid sudden inputs, give extra braking distance.
