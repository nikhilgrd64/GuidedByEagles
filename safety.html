🛡️ Essential Travel Safety Tips

Adventure is thrilling, but your safety comes first. Here’s how to stay secure and confident during your journeys.

📍 Pre-Travel Safety

  • ✅ Share your itinerary with a friend or family member.
  • ✅ Keep digital & physical copies of important documents (passport, ID, insurance).
  • ✅ Learn basic local phrases for emergencies.

🧭 On-Route Awareness

  • ✅ Stay alert in unfamiliar surroundings—especially at night.
  • ✅ Keep valuables hidden and use anti-theft bags.
  • ✅ Don’t share your exact location publicly in real-time.

🏥 Health & Emergency Prep

  • ✅ Carry a compact first-aid kit & essential meds.
  • ✅ Know the local emergency number and hospital location.
  • ✅ Stay hydrated and rest when needed.
