Travel Budgeting Tips

✦ Plan Your Budget

  • 🏨 Accommodation: 30-40%
  • 🍛 Food & Water: 20%
  • 🚌 Transport: 20-30%
  • 🎟️ Experiences & Entry Fees: 10%
  • 🆘 Emergency & Misc: 10%

✦ Money-Saving Hacks

  • Use public transport or shared rides
  • Travel in shoulder/off-seasons
  • Stay in hostels or homestays
  • Carry reusable water bottle & snacks

✦ Currency & Safety

  • Keep some local currency and 2 cards (1 backup)
  • Use currency converter apps
  • Separate your money in different bags
  • Let someone at home know your plans
